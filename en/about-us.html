Our Mission

Transform the way in which software development is seen by producing perfectly good working systems that make people happy.

Our Vision

A world where software helps people have simpler lives.

We love what we do!

Freedom

We believe that everyone at HE:labs is free to express themselves, disagree, suggest or change at any particular moment. We encourage discussions between every member of the team to seek the best solution for your project.

Transparency

Our relations are guided by transparency while always respecting everyone’s privacy. We do not worry about hiding our numbers and are transparent about the company’s real situation every moment.

Autonomy

Our members are free to choose how to deal with the projects. We don't have dressing codes and everyone can work remotely. Our teams are self-organized and the communication between them and the client is direct, without middlemen.

Continual Improvement

We are aware that work needs to be done in an iterative and incremental fashion to achieve the best results. We use our retrospective meetings to identify areas of improvement, so we are able to act fast and develop outstanding products.

Colaboration

Collaboration is essential for effective teamwork. Our collaborative spirit goes beyond HE:labs' boundaries, since we are always involved in the development community by sponsoring events, giving talks and creating open source tools.

Happiness

We love what we do and our main goal is to make our clients and their users happy by delivering the most functional and well designed product. We always ask our clients if, beyond satisfied, they are happy with our work. To us, happiness is the most relevant aspect of a job well done.
